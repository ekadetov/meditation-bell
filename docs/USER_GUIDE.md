## üîí Privacy & Data Control

### Local-Only Storage
**Your data never leaves your device.**

- All session data stored in browser IndexedDB
- AI insights generated locally (no server processing)
- No accounts, no login, no tracking
- Works completely offline

### GDPR Compliance
Full control over your personal data.

**Privacy Settings (üîí Privacy tab):**

1. **Export Your Data**
   - Download all sessions and insights as JSON
   - Includes mood history, session details, and AI insights
   - Use for backups or migration to another device

2. **Delete All Data**
   - Permanently remove all meditation history
   - Clears sessions, moods, and insights
   - Requires confirmation to prevent accidents

3. **Auto-Delete Old Data**
   - Set retention period (30, 90, 180, 365 days, or never)
   - Automatically removes data older than selected period
   - Helps manage storage space

### Data Transparency

- **Session Data**: timestamp, duration, mode, bell count

- **Preferences**: timer settings, audio volume
- **AI Insights**: generated patterns and suggestions

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can anyone else see my meditation data?**
A: No. All data stays on your device. We don't have servers to store or access your information.

**Q: What happens if I clear my browser data?**
A: Your meditation history will be deleted. Export your data first if you want to keep it.

**Q: Do I need an internet connection?**
A: Only for the initial install. After that, everything works offline.

**Q: How is AI generating insights without internet?**
A: Simple pattern detection runs in your browser JavaScript. No cloud AI services used.

**Q: Can I use this on multiple devices?**
A: Data doesn't sync automatically. Export from one device and import on another (future feature).

**Q: Does this drain my battery?**
A: No more than any timer app. The app is optimized for low power usage.
